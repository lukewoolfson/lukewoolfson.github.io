Statement of Case

You will need to include what has happened so far, personal information including the parties involved, their relationship and or children. Please provide details of these and explain why these current proceedings are necessary.

Help Note:

For substantive applications, this section should be used for the statement in support of funding, although it will be possible to upload a separate statement in addition if necessary.
For emergency applications, this section must be used for the statement in support of funding as you will be unable to upload a separate document in emergency applications.
Each text box is limited to 8000 characters (approximately 1330 words). If this is insufficient please click "Add" below for additional space.
